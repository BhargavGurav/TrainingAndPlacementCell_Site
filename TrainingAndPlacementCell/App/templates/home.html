Welcome to the Training and Placement Section, GCOE, Jalgaon

Government College of Engineering, Jalgaon is one of the premier engineering College of North Maharashtra University, maintaining an excellent academic record. The Institute's Training and Placement Section provides a launch pad for students to make it to the best organizations in the fields of software, core industries, management, etc.
Our students have lived up to the expectations of organizations that visit the campus, looking for bright candidates to take up important and challenging responsibilities at their workplace. The TAPS boasts of maintaining an excellent rapport with a wide spectrum of companies spread across various industry sectors.
The Training and Placement cell is headed by Shri. M. S. Sadavarte(TPO) who is keen on encouraging students to prefer jobs that better suit their profile. The T&P cell has adequate infrastructure to accommodate all the activities related to the training and placement of GCOEJ students.
